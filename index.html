Ao Oni

1. Story:

The story of takeshi and his friends turned from a great adventures to a disturbing story. takeshi accompanied by three friends Mika, Takuro and Hiroshi was exploring an abandoned mansion near a cliff where monsters were sighted. takeshi(the character we play as) heard glass breaking near the east side of the house. they splitted up and soon met the monster Ao Oni, this monster was huge colored by its iconic blueberry color it chases of everyone soon after Mika was caught. turns out Ao oni has developed a power to disguise as other people gaining trust by the two friends and being succsesful in killing all of takeshi's friends.

2. Gameplay:

the player basicly tries to save his friends and escaping by doing puzzels. if you do reach the end you eventually escape but alone
